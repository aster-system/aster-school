Presentation

This document it's a TV ad for the Sainsbury's products. It happens during the Christmas 1914 Truce, some soldiers from enemies countries played together. The message is that Christmas is for sharing, and not fighting. The goal of the message is to make an emotive vision of soldier for sharing. As any ads should have, it contains emotions, a good story telling and a way to introduce the product. It also uses the art of cinema.
